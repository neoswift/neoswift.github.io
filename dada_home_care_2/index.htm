Welcome to Dada Home Care, Inc.

Dada Home Care, Inc. – a company you can trust like family! We are a home health care provider that offers excellent, affordable and flexible home health care services. Our home health care staff are licensed and highly experienced in handling different people with different disabilities as well as various levels of medical-fragility. Our dedicated and compassionate Staff are always ready to serve you no matter how unique or difficult your health situation may be.

From the beginning, Dada Home Care, Inc. has been making daily living challenges easier for its clients all over the community. Our specialization is in providing attendant care services and assistance with daily living tasks, wound care and post-surgery care. We hope to provide the optimum level of service so that our patients will be well on their way to recovering from their illnesses. Our home health care personnel do not only provide medical assistance to their patients; they also provide social and emotional support with compassion and understanding. We are here for you! Together, we can make life at home easier.

If you are in need of home care in the hands of a professional, contact us now at 214-678-1950.
